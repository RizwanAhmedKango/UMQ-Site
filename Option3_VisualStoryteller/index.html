A Journey of Innovation

"My research is about understanding the language of sensors and the intelligence of the edge."

As a Senior Lecturer at James Cook University, Dr. Qureshi leads high-impact research into Industrial IoT (IIoT). His work spans the deployment of secure wireless networks to the integration of AI in precision agriculture.

Before JCU, he contributed to the academic landscapes of Hong Kong at CUHK and PolyU, and began his journey at Mehran University. This global perspective fuels his drive for cross-border collaboration and technological breakthrough.
