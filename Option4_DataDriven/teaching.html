Teaching Philosophy

My teaching philosophy is rooted in the belief that an active, problem-based approach to learning is the most effective way to prepare students for the rapidly evolving tech industry. By bridging academic theories with project-based work, I aim to equip students with both technical proficiency and critical thinking skills.
