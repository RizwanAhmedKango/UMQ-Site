Umair Mujtaba Qureshi

Senior Lecturer, Information Technology · James Cook University

umair.qureshi@jcu.edu.au Google Scholar LinkedIn ORCID CV

I am a Senior Lecturer in the Information Technology department at James Cook University, Australia. My research sits at the intersection of Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT), applied artificial intelligence, and information security.

With over a decade of experience across academia and industry in Pakistan, Hong Kong, and Australia, I lead projects that use data analytics and wireless sensor networks to solve problems in connected and autonomous systems, agricultural technology, and smart environmental monitoring. My work has accumulated 1,076 citations (h-index 17, i10-index 30).
