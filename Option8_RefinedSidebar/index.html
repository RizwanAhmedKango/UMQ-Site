James Cook University, Australia — Industrial IoT, applied AI & information security.

I am a Senior Lecturer in the Information Technology department at James Cook University (JCU). My research sits at the intersection of Industrial IoT, applied artificial intelligence, and information security.

With over a decade of experience across academia and industry, I lead projects that use data analytics and wireless sensor networks to solve problems in connected and autonomous systems, agricultural technology, and smart environmental monitoring.
