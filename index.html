Dr. Umair Mujtaba Qureshi

I am a Senior Lecturer in the Information Technology department at James Cook University (JCU), Australia. My research focus lies at the intersection of Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT), Applied Artificial Intelligence, and Information Security.

With over a decade of experience across academia and industry, I lead research projects that leverage data analytics and wireless sensor networks to solve complex problems in connected and autonomous systems, agricultural technology, and smart environmental monitoring.
